Niagara Falls 2 days trip from NYC

Price: $700

Date: December 2025

Driving Tour Plan: NYC to Niagara Falls (2 Days, 1 Night)

Day 1: NYC to Niagara Falls

**Depart NYC:** - Begin your adventure with excitement as you drive toward Niagara Falls. **Lunch Stop:** - Enjoy a relaxing lunch in Erie, PA, soaking in local flavors. **Niagara Falls Exploration:** - Arrive at Niagara Falls State Park. Experience breathtaking views of American Falls and Bridal Veil Falls while taking a scenic walk along the pathways. **Evening Activities:** - Check into an Airbnb house for a cozy and unique stay. - Enjoy a warm BBQ homemade dinner at your Airbnb, perfect for winding down after a day of exploration.

#### Day 2: Discover Niagara Falls

**Morning Adventures:** - Start your day with breakfast in your Airbnb. - Take the thrilling **Maid of the Mist** boat ride, getting up close to the falls and feeling the mist on your face. **Cave of the Winds:** - Experience the Cave of the Winds, standing just feet away from the thundering water. **Lunch with a View:** - Enjoy a meal at a restaurant with stunning views of the falls. **Afternoon Discoveries:** - Visit the observation tower for panoramic views, then stroll across the Rainbow Bridge for a unique perspective of the falls. **Return to NYC:** - Head back, reflecting on the memories created during your journey.
